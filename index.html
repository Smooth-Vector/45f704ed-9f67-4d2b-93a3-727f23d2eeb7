About Her Trowel

Passion for the craft

More than just a trade — plastering is about transforming spaces. Every home tells a story, and the walls should do it justice.

Jessica Shrimpton
Founder & Plasterer

I started Her Trowel after years of refining my skills on projects across Suffolk. Plastering is something I genuinely love — the transformation of a rough wall into a perfectly smooth, paint-ready finish is incredibly satisfying.

Based right here in Hadleigh, I work with local homeowners and tradespeople to deliver high-quality results at honest prices. No rushing, no cutting corners — just excellent, careful work that I'm proud to put my name to.

What I Offer

Services

Skim Plastering

A smooth, thin coat applied over existing plaster or dry board to create a flawless, paint-ready surface.

Patch Repairs

Seamless repairs to damaged or cracked plaster, matched to the existing finish so no one would ever know.

New Build & Renovation

Full plastering packages for new builds and whole-house renovations, working alongside other trades.

Lime Plastering

Traditional lime plaster for breathable, period-appropriate finishes — ideal for older properties and natural materials.
