What I Offer

Services

Skim Plastering

A smooth, thin coat applied over existing plaster or dry board to create a flawless, paint-ready surface.

Patch Repairs

Seamless repairs to damaged or cracked plaster, matched to the existing finish so no one would ever know.

Artex Removal

Careful removal of old textured ceilings and walls, skimmed flat and ready for modern decorating.

New Build & Renovation

Full plastering packages for new builds and whole-house renovations, working alongside other trades.
